Positive Achievements
of Our Stream



What were the things well done during the stream?

One of the strengths of the stream was the way the hosts communicated and interacted during gameplay. Viewers specifically enjoyed moments when everyone was talking and panicking while playing games like Expedition Antarctica and Running from the Internet. These games created a fun and lively atmosphere that kept the audience entertained. Another positive aspect was the audio quality; most respondents said everyone was audible, which made it easy to follow the conversations and reactions during the stream.
