Area of Opportunity



What are the areas of opportunities for your stream?

The stream could improve in a few key areas. First, the level of interaction with the chat was not consistent, only 40% of viewers felt that there was enough interaction, while the rest were unsure or said no. Second, some viewers mentioned that the stream became boring when the hosts were looking for a game or when they stopped talking. This suggests a need to minimize downtime and maintain constant engagement. Technical problems and an irregular streaming schedule were also mentioned as weaknesses, which could be addressed by improving equipment setup and sticking to a consistent time. Finally, viewers expressed interest in more variety and topics, which means there is room to explore different types of content beyond just gameplay.
